Bacon recipe

Description

Bacon is a type of salt-cured pork made from various cuts, typically the belly or less fatty parts of the back. It is eaten as a side dish, used as a central ingredient, or as a flavouring or accent. The recipe I am following is for candied bacon which, let's face it, is just better than other bacon. Fun fact about bacon it is a know carcinogen, probably why it is so cheap. With this in mind keep consumption of bacon to an absolute minimum.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 pound thick-cut bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix brown sugar, rice vinegar, and maple syrup in a small bowl.
  3. Place bacon slices on a cooling rack set over a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 minutes, turn slices, and bake another 5 minutes.
  5. Remove bacon and brush both sides with brown sugar mixture. Return bacon to the oven and bake another 5 minutes. Repeat basting every 5 minutes until bacon is browned and crisp, about 35 minutes.
  6. Once finished, place bacon on plate and let it cool for 5 minutes.
  7. Now enjoy!
